Welcome!

The name's Alex Koff. I am a hobbyist, self-taught DJ from Klaipeda, Lithuania.

I have been active in this passion for the past 6 years.

I started out with the trance genre back in 2013,

and worked my way up through several different genres,

including most sub-genres of house, drum and bass, future funk, electro swing, techno, and many others.

Wander around! Take a look (or a listen) at my work.

The "Mixes" page contains my two most prominent mix series,

while the "Albums" page holds the mashups and remixes done by me.

If you wanna get in touch, the "Contacts" page is all you need!

Happy wandering!
